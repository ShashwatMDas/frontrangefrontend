What We’re About

Front Range Front-End is a meetup for, well, front-end developers. We focus on:

  • HTML: accessibility, semantic markup
  • CSS: new techniques, tools like PostCSS & Sass
  • JS: vanilla JS, browser APIs, not frameworks

Where We Meet

Our meetup is hosted by the kind folks at Code. You can find us at:

Code Talent

When We Meet

We meet the first Thursday of every month, at 6:30pm.

We don’t have food provided currently, but if the group grows & we can get some sponsors, we’d be happy to add that.
